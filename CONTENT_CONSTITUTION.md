# Content Constitution


## Word count and outline

- Hard word count: **800–2000** words.
- Target band: **1050–1400** words.
- H2 outline is mandatory and must match exactly (no extra H2s).

### Required H2 outline

- Intro
- Definitions and key terms
- Why this topic exists
- What this feeling or situation usually means
- How people usually experience this
- Common reasons this happens
- How it typically works
- What tends to make it worse
- What helps (non-advice)
- When this topic tends to come up
- Clarifying examples
- Common misconceptions
- Why this topic gets misunderstood online
- When it might signal a bigger issue
- Related situations that feel similar
- Related topics and deeper reading
- Neutral summary
- FAQs
